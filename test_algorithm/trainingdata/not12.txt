U-M Ann Arbor All-Day Sacred Harp Singing Join more than 100 singers to give voice to the earliest choral music tradition in America’s history. The students of Prof. Mark Clague’s musicology class host a traditional Sacred Harp singing. Singing starts at 10am and 1:30pm with a 1-hour lunch break at 12:30. Singers from throughout Illinois, Ohio, Indiana and even as far as Georgia and Canada are expected. Registration (and coffee!) begins at 9am.
