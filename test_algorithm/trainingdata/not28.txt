The 2016 eight-home game season kicks off on September 3 vs. Hawaii. Students who purchase football season tickets will also receive a FREE t-shirt that may be picked up at any MDen store in late August using the voucher provided with your season tickets. Football away game student ticket orders will be accepted in person at the Athletic Ticket Office April 13-15 from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Only student football season ticket purchasers may apply, and there is a limit of 1 ticket per student per game.
